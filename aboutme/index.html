Bien Venidos - Welcome to My-Info-Page! I went to college a long time ago when computer science was relatively new (punch-cards, assemblers and compilers), and am now working on a refresh degree of knowledge and skills that are relevant, applicable, fulfilling and fun.

I grew up at the Grand Canyon and graduated high-school there, after which I attended college, served a mission to Argentina, attended more college and started a family with my wife Kris. We have 2 children and 2 granddaughters.

Currently I work with solar power and energy storage where I lead a team of designers and engineers using AutoCAD to produce drawings and specifications for renewable energy projects. Historically my work has been a combination of engineering, design, and project management for projects in the US and also abroad.

It is exciting to learn more about coding for web development and applications avaiable for ongoing and future business opportunities.
